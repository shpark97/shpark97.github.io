About Me
------

Hello, I'm Seonghwan Park, a master student in Artificial Intelligence at [POSTECH](https://ai.postech.ac.kr/).

I am advised by [Prof. Namhoon Lee](https://namhoonlee.github.io/), in the [Lee Optimization Group (LOG) Lab](https://namhoonlee.github.io/group). I'm currently interested in efficient federated/distributed learning and large-scale language model compression.
